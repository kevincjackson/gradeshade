About

Gradeshade is a *visual* grade calculator.

Put in your assignments, weights, and grades and the graph on the right shows how many points you earned for each assignment, while the top of the page gives you the final grade. For example,

  • A perfect 100% in the class shows a 100% filled square and an "A" up top.
  • A 90% in the class shows a 90% filled square and an "A-" up top.
  • and so on...

Try clicking on the "Example" buttons above to see what some entered grades look like.
